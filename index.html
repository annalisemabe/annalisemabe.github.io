    • About Me

    Annalise Mabe is completing an MFA in Creative Writing at the University of South Florida where she writes Nonfiction, Poetry, and Comics. She reads for Sweet: A Literary Confection and is a poetry and art/graphic editor at Saw Palm: Florida Literature and Art. She teaches creative writing and compostion at USF and is currently working on a chapbook of poetry and drafting her first memoir.

    She can be reached at annalisemabe@mail.usf.edu or on Twitter: @AnnaliseMabe
